Product Description
Use these task cards instead of worksheets to get students working with parallel lines cut by a transversals and exterior and interior angles of a triangle.

These task cards support 8th grade math standards CCSS 8.G.A.5 and TEKS 8.8D

Choose from a variety of engaging task card activities to meet the needs of the learners in your classroom.

Included in this download:

-Teacher Directions and Tips for Differentiation
-10 Engaging Low Prep Ways to Use Task Cards
-Parallel Lines Cut by a Transversal and Angles of a Triangle Task Cards- 24 cards which can be printed with answers on the back (print double-sided) for self-correcting.
-Student Record Sheets for students to record responses (2 pages)
-Answer key- 2 pages. For use when cards aren’t double –sided with correct answers.
-Envelope for storage of game cards.
